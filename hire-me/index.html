the deal.

I am available for remote-only, contract based software development work.

I work very fast and write top-quality code, tests and documentation. My rates are a reflection of that promise.

My hourly rate ranges from $100/h to $150/h depending on the type of work I am doing.

Static HTML/CSS/JS work is typically less expensive. Rich interactive work is somewhere in the middle and full-stack work is towards the higher end. Algorithm design, analysis, and data architecture occupy the highest end of that range.

I no longer accept fixed bid work and all projects must have a minimum value of $10K to make ramp up worth my time.

I require a discovery period of at least one week for all jobs. Discovery is billed at its own rate of $90 / hour.
